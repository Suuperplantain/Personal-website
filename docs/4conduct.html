Reflection on Professional Conduct

1. Professional Conduct for Students

Definition and Application

Professional conduct as a student revolves around upholding academic integrity, a cornerstone of NTU's policies. This ensures scholarly work is honest, original, and respectful of intellectual property.

Practical Implementation:
Routinely using Turnitin via NTU's Dropbox to check for accidental plagiarism, ensuring submissions reflect genuine effort and adherence to ethical standards.

In group projects, professionalism extends to:

  • Completing assigned tasks (coding modules/research) without delays
  • Using WhatsApp/Teams for coordination when in-person collaboration isn't possible

2. Impact of Unprofessional Behavior

Consequences and Personal Insight

Unprofessional actions disrupt group dynamics and compromise outcomes. A team member delaying work forces others to compensate hastily, lowering quality and increasing stress.

Personal Experience:
A peer's last-minute notification about unfinished tasks left our group scrambling to revise code/documentation, risking errors and reduced marks.
